Direction: Each item in this section consists of statements followed by several possible conclusions. Determine the best conclusion that can be logically made from the given statements.

1) A rise in global temperatures due to climate change is leading to more frequent and severe natural disasters, such as floods, hurricanes, and wildfires.

A) Climate change is the primary cause of the increasing frequency of natural disasters around the world.
B) Natural disasters are increasing in frequency and severity, but they are unrelated to global temperature rise.
C) Human activities that contribute to global warming are directly linked to the increase in natural disasters.
D) Global warming has no impact on the frequency or intensity of natural disasters.
E) None of the above.

2) If they study hard and continuously, then they can score good marks.

A) Studying hard and continuously guarantees that they will always score good marks.
B) Scoring good marks is impossible without studying hard.
C) Hard work and continuous study will help them score good marks, but it's not a certainty.
D) Studying hard and continuously may sometimes result in poor marks.
E) They can score good marks without studying hard or continuously.

3) She will achieve success if she works hard.

A) Working hard is how she will attain success.
B) Even if she does not work hard, she will achieve success.
C) She will achieve success only if she works hard and not through any other means.
D) Working hard will guarantee her success, no matter what.
E) Success is impossible without working hard.

4) Iron fortified salt could reduce anemia.

A) A new process enables ordinary table salt to be fortified with iron.
B) This advance could help reduce the high incidence of anemia in the world's population due to a deficiency of iron in the diet.
C) Salt is used as a preservative for food and a flavor enhancer all over the globe, and
D) People consume salt in quantities that would provide iron in significant amounts.
E) None of the above.

5) Jade plant needs little water, and it has thick leaves.

A) All plants having thick leaves need little water.
B) Only jade plants can grow in places with little water.
C) The jade plant needs more water than most other plants.
D) Plants with thick leaves will always survive without water.
E) Jade plants may grow in places where water is not abundant.
